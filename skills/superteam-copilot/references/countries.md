## Notes

- **Superteam Balkan** is a multi-country chapter covering the Balkan region (11 countries): Albania, Bosnia, Bulgaria, Croatia, Kosovo, Montenegro, North Macedonia, Romania, Serbia, Slovenia, Greece
- **La Familia** is the Spanish-speaking community
- **Japan and Korea** are East Asia, not Southeast Asia — for SEA-specific questions, the relevant chapters are Indonesia, Singapore, and Malaysia
- Chapter tracks in the Frontier Hackathon can be identified by the `sponsor.chapter` field being non-null in the Superteam API response
- Total: 23 Superteam communities globally (as of April 2026)
