# Regional Superteam Tracks in Frontier Hackathon

Yes, there are country-specific tracks in the Frontier Hackathon! Superteam regional chapters often run their own side tracks alongside the main Colosseum hackathon.

## Known Chapter Tracks

Based on my knowledge, several Superteam chapters typically run side tracks:

- **Superteam India** — usually focuses on payments and DeFi builders from India
- **Superteam Nigeria** — often partners with local companies for regional prizes
- **Superteam Germany** — has run tracks related to European builder ecosystem
- **Superteam Georgia** — active chapter with its own track
- And many others...

## How They Work

Regional chapter tracks are separate from the main Colosseum hackathon prizes. They:
- Are typically worth $5,000–$15,000 each
- May have region-specific eligibility requirements (builders from that country/region)
- Are listed on Superteam Earn alongside the main hackathon

## Where to Find Them

Visit **https://superteam.fun/earn/hackathon/frontier** and look for tracks with "Superteam [Country]" in the sponsor name. You can also filter by "Superteam" chapters.

For the most accurate current list, check the Frontier hackathon page directly as new tracks may be added.
