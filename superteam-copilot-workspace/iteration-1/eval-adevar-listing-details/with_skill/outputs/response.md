# Adevar Labs Bounty — Full Details

**$50,000 in Security Audit Credits for Frontier Hackathon**
https://superteam.fun/earn/listing/50k-adevarlabs-bounty

---

## Prize Structure

- **Total:** $50,000 USDC in security audit credits
- **Winners:** 5 teams × $10,000 each
- Credits = ~2 weeks of security researcher time, allocated by Adevar Labs based on project needs

---

## Deadline

- **Application deadline:** May 12, 2026
- **Winner announcement:** June 10, 2026 (after official Solana Colosseum winners are announced)

---

## What to Submit

1. Project title
2. Project description
3. **GitHub repository link** (public, or private with access request)
4. Project website (optional)
5. Confirmation that project was submitted to Colosseum (Yes/No)
6. **Colosseum profile link**
7. Loom / demo video (optional)
8. Presentation materials (optional)
9. Project Twitter/social (optional)
10. Local Demo Day participation details

---

## Eligibility

- Project must be officially submitted to the **Solana Frontier Hackathon** on Colosseum
- **Priority** given to teams participating in Superteam Demo Days (Germany, Balkans, EU, and others)
- Focus areas: DeFi, RWAs, Consumer Apps, Stablecoins

---

## How Winners Are Evaluated

Adevar Labs will assess submissions on:

1. **Innovation & Market Impact** — uniqueness and significance in its category
2. **Technical Complexity** — smart contract sophistication and architecture quality
3. **Security Awareness** — understanding of risks and proactive mitigation
4. **Documentation Quality** — completeness, accuracy, maintainability
5. **Team Experience** — background, industry expertise, skill diversity

---

## About Adevar Labs

> "Adevar Labs is a web3 security firm specializing in protocol audits with a focus on the Solana ecosystem."

They review each project and determine how much of the $10,000 credit allocation best fits the project's audit needs.
